CULTURAL:

The Himadri hostel has always proven to have a strong cultural standing housing some of the best debaters, dancers, singers, painters and many more. The hostel has 9 cultural clubs with a representative working towards the development of each all year around. The clubs come under the guidance and vigilance of the cultural secretary. If you ever have any problem or query related to any of the clubs mentioned below, feel free to contact any of the representatives or the cultural secretary herself.

1. SPIC MACAY: NAME OF REP

SPICMACAY stands for Society for the Promotion of Indian Classical Music And Culture Amongst Youth. As the name suggests, its main aim is to create awareness about India’s rich cultural diversity among the youth. Every year, eminent artists from various fields give performances. Last year as a part of the “Virasat” series we witnessed the performances of L. Subramaniam (Carnatic Violin), Prerara Shrimali (Kathak), Hariprasad Chaurasia (Flute) to name a few. Also, even students were given opportunities to showcase their talent to the nation. Also, many workshops such as calligraphy workshop, kathak workshop etc. are organized to engage students in fun learning activities.

2. PHOTOGRAPHY AND FILMS CLUB: NAME OF REP

Photography is the Story that you fail to put into word. We at PFC would not only help you to improve your photography and editing skills but would also let you learn from the very basics. From using a DSLR to pro video editing everything. Come and join this ever-growing community of editor and designers. A plethora of fun and learnings wait for you…

3. MUSIC CLUB: NAME OF REP

Music is a part of all our lives.You need not be a great singer to experience the joys of music.Music binds people to each other. We here at Himadri celebrate our togetherness as a family with music. Come be a part of the himadri music club and rise up to the stage where you get the loud applause

4. Literary Club

If you love to read a good story, LitSoc is the place for you! Be it reading, writing, poetry or quizzing, the club conducts workshops and competitions in all spheres related to the literary world. Moreover, we offer you a platform to meet other book-lovers in campus and discuss your favorites in the Bring Your Own Book sessions and Reader Circle meetings. Do look out for Potpourri, our first inter-hostel event, and celebrate the joy of book-reading!

5. FACC

Art can be anything from being an opening to creativity, the solitude where you let your mind unfold, a recreation to enjoy with your friends or a showcase for skill and talent. Art is anything you want it to be. The Fine Arts and Crafts Club gives you an opportunity to try things you’ve never done before- from wall paintings and street art, charcoal and oil paintings to carving, origami or even just doodling. With fun filled workshops to learn from and exhibitions and competitions there will be plenty of opportunities for you to find yourself or lose yourself in art. So don’t pull back, express yourself and unleash your creativity here at Himadri.

6. HINDI SAMITI:

भारत की विश्व भर में पहचान हमारी संस्कृति और भाषा से होती है। हिंदी भाषा ने भारत में एकता अखंडता को बनाए रखा है परंतु पश्चिमी देशों की जीवनशैली को अपनाने के पीछे हम अपनी ही मातृभाषा को भूलते जा रहे हैं। हिंदी का अधिपत्य कायम करने के लिए हिंदी समिति महत्वपूर्ण भूमिका निभा रहा है।विभिन्न प्रकार की प्रतियोगिताएँ जैसे अन्दाज़-ए-बयान, अनुवाद, हिंदी स्क्रैबल, बॉलीवुड क्विज़, आदि आयोजित कराई जाती है।

MAINTENANCE:

WE WON SOME AWARD FOR THIS, RIGHT?

The Himadri Hostel is well equipped with all new age maintenance facilities, present for the sole purpose to make the life of the residents as homely as possible. Here are the facilities that Himadri currently offers: Computer room: Equipped with X working desktops, any resident who wishes to use the computers to study, access the internet or leisurely activities can ask for the keys at the reception and use the computer room to the best of it’s capacity. Common room: The common room is an air-conditioned room with sofas and television for lounging and spending quality time with friends away from their rooms. The room also is the hub for all cultural workshops that are taken for the students. Library: a quiet place for people to study and indulge in book reading, this air conditioned library has a plethora of fiction and non- fiction titles neatly stacked in cabinets. The room has a set of wide tables and chairs and can be used 24*7. Gym (see sports): A healthy lifestyle requires both, a healthy diet and regular exercise. The gym has multiple machines and equipment to aid the students in their excercise routine. This spacious, well-lit room can also, just all the others, be accessed 24*7. Laundry room: the hostel has two laundry rooms, one on the ground floor and another one on the first floor. Each of the rooms has about 5 automatic or semi-automatic washing machines. To use them, you just have to make an entry in the laundry room register at the reception and you can wash your clothes and other suitable items in accordance with your own time. Visitor’s room: Right across the reception, this air-conditioned room is for any and all the visitors which come to meet the residents. Male visitors can stay here till only 10:00 p.m. to bring any of your visitors into this room simply make an entry at the entry gates at the time of entering and leaving. Garden: The Himadri hostel, just like all parts of the campus, is surrounded by lush greenery all around. A gardener is present on the hostel grounds during working hours and tends to the garden bed surrounding the entrance and swings. The maintenance committee works tirelessly towards making sure that the facilities are always available to all the residents present. In case of any help, you are most welcome to contact any of these members: Maintenance Secretary: Aakriti Singh Garden and Laundry room Secretary: Uppadi Roshini Sai Library Secretary: Vidushi Toshniwal Here at himadri, we are working to develop the reading environment. As a Library Secretary, I want to assure that everyone gets a proper place and environment to study. Along with this we have established a system where people can share books that they have among each other so.that a healthy reading environment will built up. We also plan to provide some bestselling novels for the hostel library. Have a happy reading! Common/ Computer Room secretary: Krishna Chaudhary
